About Me

I'm a second-year PhD Student in the Computer Science department at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I'm a member of the Automated Software Engineering (ASE) group, and am advised by Professor Tao Xie.

My research interests are broadly distributed in the field of Software Engineering. I've done work in text analytics for feature location and mobile app security. My current work involves software test generation and symbolic execution.
